About Me

01

Profile

Graduate researcher in electrical engineering with a strong background in embedded systems, FPGA design, and high-speed data acquisition. Research and development efforts are focused on real-time signal processing systems that tightly integrate hardware and software to achieve ultra-low latency and high throughput.

02

Projects

Currently developing a high-speed Wi‑Fi 6 data logger on Zynq UltraScale+ with PetaLinux for real-time edge data acquisition, alongside FPGA-based AI inference for signal processing. Projects span full-stack design—from custom sensor drivers (e.g. AD7768, ISL26104) to Qt-based GUIs—with published results in academic venues.
